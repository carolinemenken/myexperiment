In this study you will make a choice between two jobs. You will see information about each job. The information you see is:

  • Wage: shows how much you would get paid for the job on basis of working full time
  • Hours: shows if the job is full time (so 40 hours) or flexible (24-36 hours)
  • Distance: shows how long it will take to get to the job
  • Useful for society: shows if the job will have a meaningful impact on the society
  • Advance opportunities : shows if it is possible to grow within 10 years in the organization

    • You can see this information by moving your mouse over the box

    You can make a decision by pressing on option A or option B

    The experiment consists of 18 rounds
